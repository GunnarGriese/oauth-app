Best Practices

These filters should be present in your view to achieve high quality data:

  • IP filter (exclude internal and agency traffic)
  • Hostname filter (include only traffic to the relevant domain)
  • Keep in mind that filters are executed in a subsequent order (indicated by the rank). Pay attention to any dependencies that might occur!
